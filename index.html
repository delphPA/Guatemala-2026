Good to Know

Currency
Guatemalan Quetzal (GTQ). Carry cash — many places don't take cards.
Getting Around
Private shuttle from GUA. Lanchas on the lake. Tuk-tuks in Panajachel.
Swimming
Laguna Lodge has an infinity pool + lake swimming with snorkels. Kayaks to borrow. Go before noon — afternoon wind makes lake choppy.
Food
Laguna Lodge serves breakfast + 4-course vegetarian dinner. Restaurant on site. Eat meat in nearby villages.

Only ~2hrs between UA arrival (4:37pm) and TAG departure (6:50pm). Move fast through customs!

What to Wear Each Day
Apr 2–4
Tikal

☀️ Hot jungle days · Cool pre-dawn

Lightweight short sleeves and shorts during the day. For the 4am sunrise tour: long sleeves + light layer (it will feel cool in the dark). Closed shoes for jungle walking. Sunglasses and hat essential.

Apr 4–9
Atitlán

🌤️ Warm days · Cool nights · Possible afternoon showers

T-shirts and light layers during the day. A fleece or light jacket for evenings and boat rides — the lake breeze makes it feel colder. One waterproof layer in case of a short afternoon shower. Comfortable walking shoes for village visits.
