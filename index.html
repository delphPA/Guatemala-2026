Apr 4

Arrive · Lancha to Santa Cruz · Settle in

Private transfer from GUA airport to Panajachel (~3hrs). Take a lancha (water taxi) across to Santa Cruz La Laguna. Check in from 3PM. First sunset over the three volcanoes. 🌋

Apr 5

🎿 Zipline at Reserva Natural Atitlán

Book the 9am tour in Panajachel — 9 cables over the valley with volcano views, butterfly geodome, hanging bridges and spider monkeys. Back to the lodge for a peaceful afternoon.

atitlanreserva.com · +502 7762 2565
Apr 6

🚤 Lake Villages by Boat · Room move to Ceiba Suite

Morning lancha tour with Voltan Adventures: San Juan la Laguna (weaving cooperatives, murals) and San Pedro la Laguna (kayak on the lake). Check into the Ceiba Suite from 3PM — upgrade! 🎉

voltanadventures.com
Apr 7

🛶 Free day — your choice

Options: kayak to a nearby village, hike Indian Nose for sunrise views, paddleboard on the lake, yoga at the lodge, or simply sit with volcano views and do nothing. You've earned it.

Apr 8

🏘️ Santiago Atitlán + Maximón shrine

Take a lancha to Santiago Atitlán — visit the famous Maximón shrine, a uniquely Guatemalan cultural experience Jeremy won't forget. Browse the local market. Last evening watching the volcanoes at sunset.

Apr 9

Good to Know

Currency
Guatemalan Quetzal (GTQ). Carry cash — many places don't take cards.
Weather
April = end of dry season. Hot & sunny. Sunscreen, hat, light layers for lake evenings.
Getting Around
Private shuttle from GUA. Lanchas on the lake between villages. Tuk-tuks in Panajachel.
Health
Mosquito repellent (jungle). Bottled water only. Travel insurance recommended.
