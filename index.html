Good to Know

Currency
Guatemalan Quetzal (GTQ). Carry cash — many places don't take cards.
Weather
April = end of dry season. Hot & sunny. Pack sunscreen, hat, light layers for evenings.
Getting Around
Private shuttles between destinations. Tuk-tuks locally. Lanchas on the lake.
Health
Mosquito repellent (jungle + coast). Bottled water only. Travel insurance recommended.
Language
Spanish. Most tourist areas have English speakers. A few phrases go a long way!
⚠️ IAH Warning
Terminal change at Houston on Apr 9 return. Allow extra time between UA 1911 → UA 1609.
