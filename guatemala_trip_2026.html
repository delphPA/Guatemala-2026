Good to Know

Currency
Guatemalan Quetzal (GTQ). Carry cash — many places don't take cards.
Weather
April = end of dry season. Hot & sunny. Pack sunscreen, hat, light layers for highland evenings.
Getting Around
Private shuttles between destinations. Tuk-tuks locally. Lanchas (water taxis) on the lake.
Health
Pack mosquito repellent (jungle + coast). Drink bottled water only. Travel insurance recommended.
Language
Spanish. Most tourist areas have English speakers. A few phrases go a long way! 🙏
Flights
United: SFO → GUA via Houston. Domestic: GUA ↔ FRS (Flores) via TAG or Avianca (~1hr).
